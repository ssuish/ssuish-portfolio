# Open Source Contributions

[< Back to Projects](/projects/)

Use this page to highlight open source work, contributions to other projects, or community involvement.

## Problem

You want a central place to point people to your open source activity without listing every single commit.

## Solution

- Feature a few key projects or contributions.
- Briefly describe what you worked on and why it mattered.

## Example Structure

- **Project name** – one or two sentences about what the project does and what you contributed.
- **Role** – maintainer, contributor, occasional bug fixer, etc.
- **Notable contributions** – links to issues, PRs, or features.

## Highlights

- Show impact rather than just activity.
- Emphasize collaboration, code quality, and communication.
